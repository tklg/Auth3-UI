Resetting a password

If you've forgotten your password, click the "forgot password" link underneath the password fields. Provide the email address you use to sign in, and an email containing a recovery link will be sent to it.

Logging in to other services

Auth3 allows you to use your account to sign in to other services.
Before allowing an application to access your account, you will have a change to read over a list of information and actions the application will be allowed to access.
You are able to revoke the applications' access at any time from the account page.
